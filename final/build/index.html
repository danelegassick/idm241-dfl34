IDM241 - Final Build

Triggers

  • Hover on text in tab
  • Click on tab under favorites (middle of page)
  • Hover on image in card
  • Hover on heart icon in card
  • Click on heart icon in card

Rules

  • On hover of text in tab, cursor changes from default to pointer.
  • Off hover of text in tab, cursor changes from pointer to default.
  • On click of a new tab, the tab's green bottom border slides with an ease-in-out over 0.3s from its last position into place under the clicked tab.
  • On click of a new tab, the new tab's text is transitioned from light to bold in 0.3s with an ease-in-out.
  • On click of a new tab, the old tab's text is transitioned from bold to light in 0.3s with an ease-in-out.
  • On click of a new tab, the current tab's content cards shift down 24px, and opacity fades from 100% to 0% over 320ms
  • On click of new tab, the new content shifts up 24px as the opacity fades from 0% to 100% over 320ms, creating a crossfade effect

  • On hover of image, cursor changes from default to pointer.
  • On hover of image, image zooms in 30%.
  • Off hover of image, cursor changes from pointer to default.
  • Off hover of image, image zooms out 30%.

  • On hover of heart icon, cursor changes from default to pointer.
  • Off hover of heart icon, cursor changes from pointer to default.
  • On click of the white and black heart icon, icon expands by 50% and fills with green color with a rapid fade. After a delay the icon shrinks back down by 50%.
  • On click of the green heart icon, icon shrinks by 50% and fills with white color and black outline with a rapid fade. After a delay the icon expands back down by 50%.
