Microinteractions IDM241

Beta Description - Original Website

Triggers

  • Click on tab under favorites (middle of page)

Rules

  • On click of a new tab, the current tab's content cards shift down 24px, and opacity fades from 100% to 0%
  • On click of new tab, on a delay after the current content fades, the new content shifts up 24px as the opacity fades from 0% to 100%

Feedback

  • On click of a new tab, the current tab's content cards shift down 24px, and opacity fades from 100% to 0%
  • On click of new tab, on a delay after the current content fades, the new content shifts up 24px as the opacity fades from 0% to 100%

Loops and Modes

  • Mode: The content of the page will change based on the specific tab that is clicked
